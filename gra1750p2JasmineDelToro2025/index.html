Lasagna

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of broad, flat pasta sheets layered with fillings such as meat sauce (ragú),vegetables, and various cheeses, typically including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, and then baked.

Ingrediants

  • Ragú
  • Noodles  
  • Ricotta mixture 
  • Mozzerella 
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place the ragú at the very bottom of dish.
  2. Place the cooked noodled on top of the ragú.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture. 
  4. Place mozzerella and parmesan.
  5. Repeat as many times as needed.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes in a preheated oven at 375 degrees F. After 25 minutes take foil off.
  7. Let the lasagna sit for 15 minutes before serving.
